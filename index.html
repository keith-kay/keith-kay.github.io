About Sacco

Who we are

Grainbulk sacco is a licensed Non Withdrawal Deposit taking sacco (NWDT) established in the year 2002 with the main aim of empowering member's social economic status. Its membership is drawn from Eleven (11) companies doing business Grain Bulk Handlers Ltd.

Our Ethos

Vision

To provide financial needs to its members at affordable and attractive rates

Mission

To be an attractive financial lending institution with the aim of empowering its members living standards.

Core Values

We firmly believe in upholding corporate values as the central guiding principles that form the bedrock of our business conduct. These values are the cornerstone of our culture and serve as compass points to steer our behavior. They define what we stand for, what motivates us, and what influences our decisions, actions, and interactions with others on a daily basis. Our chosen core values reflect our unwavering commitment to integrity, purpose, and building meaningful relationships with all those we engage with.

  • Efficiency: - We prioritize streamlined operations, smart decision-making, and continuous improvement to deliver high-quality services to our members and stakeholders.Our commitment is aimed at optimizing our resources and processes to achieve maximum output with minimal waste
  • Customer satisfaction: - Our unwavering commitment is to ensure that our members and stakeholders' needs and expectations are met and exceeded. We aim to build strong and lasting relationships, foster trust, and create a positive and rewarding experience for everyone we serve.
  • Adherence to Ethical, practises, integrity, transparency, a listening & caring partner : - We strongly uphold the importance of adhering to ethical practices, promoting integrity, transparency, and being an attentive and compassionate partner.

Savings Products

Share Capital

This is a shareholding that every member MUST hold in the Sacco.

  • Minimum is Ksh. 750
  • It earns dividends
  • Can be used as a security against a loan.
  • Upon termination, It can only be transferred to another existing member as per Grainbulk Non-WDT Sacco laws.
